LMG General Contractors was founded in 2004 with a single mission: deliver exceptional construction with integrity and precision. From our Houston headquarters, we serve clients across Texas — from the energy corridor to the Rio Grande Valley.

As a Hilton, Marriott, IHG, and Hyatt approved contractor, we bring flag-standard quality to every hospitality project. Our team of experienced project managers, superintendents, and craftsmen treat every project like it's our own.
